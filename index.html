Salted Pretzel Brownie: Balancing Sweet & Savory

Rohit Mehta • May 20, 2025

Use a slightly underbaked, fudgy brownie base so the pretzels don't make it cakey. Toss pretzel pieces in a small amount of melted butter and a sprinkle of sugar before pressing some into the top of the batter — this helps them stay crisp after baking. For the caramel, cook sugar until amber, add cream and a pinch of flaky sea salt. Drizzle lightly to keep the balance between sweet and salty.

Pro Tip

If you want extra crunch on serving day, reserve a handful of pretzel pieces to scatter over the caramel just before presenting.

Matcha Cupcake

Kavya Deshpande • Apr 10, 2025

Mix matcha powder with flour.

Maple Glaze
  • Whisk together 3 tbsp maple syrup, 1 tbsp melted butter, and enough icing sugar to form a slow ribbon.
  • Pour over the warm loaf so it seeps into the crumb yet sets with a gentle sheen.
  • Finish with a pinch of smoked salt to brighten the sweetness.

Bake for 20 minutes.

About Sweet Delights Bakery

Three generations of baking excellence since 1960

Meet Our Founders

Raj Patel

Founder & Master Baker

Started Sweet Delights in 1960 with a passion for traditional Indian sweets and European baking techniques. His innovative approach to blending flavors created our signature style.

Legacy: Created our famous Rasmalai Cake recipe

Daya Patel

Co-Founder & Business Manager

Joined Raj in building Sweet Delights from a small neighborhood bakery to Mumbai's beloved institution. Her business acumen and customer service philosophy shaped our values.

Legacy: Established our "Customer First" philosophy

Our Journey Through Generations

1960

The Beginning

First Generation: Raj and Daya Patel open Sweet Delights in a small 300 sq ft shop in Bandra. Starting with just 5 traditional sweets and 3 types of bread.

1975

Expansion Era

Introduced European pastries and cakes. The famous Chocolate Truffle cake becomes Mumbai's favorite. Expanded to a 1200 sq ft space.

1988

Second Generation Takes Over

Second Generation: Arjun Patel (Raj's son) joins the business, bringing modern techniques while preserving traditional recipes. Launched custom cake services.

1995

Innovation & Quality

Introduced state-of-the-art baking equipment. Became the first bakery in Mumbai to offer sugar-free and gluten-free options.

2010

Digital Age & Expansion

Third Generation: Priya and Rohit Patel (Arjun's children) modernize operations with online ordering and home delivery services.

2020

Pandemic Resilience

Adapted quickly to contactless delivery and safety protocols. Launched DIY baking kits for families during lockdown.

2025

65 Years Strong

Today, Sweet Delights serves over 500 customers daily, with 3 generations working together, maintaining the perfect blend of tradition and innovation.
